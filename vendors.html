About

Valley Meadows Weddings & Events is the perfect venue for your highly personalized, custom wedding, reception, and/or party. The property has been a longtime favorite for family weddings and close friends’ special events, but in 2016 the Tell family will be opening their gates to the public for the first time. As an up-and-coming and 100% outdoor venue, we will offer greater flexibility than local state parks and a lower rate than most nearby private venues.

If you’re looking for the perfect blank canvas for your DIY dream event (for 300+ people!), Valley Meadows is it. Wide open Meadows, a romantic Arbor, new completed cedar Pavilion, and the brightly beautiful Bridal Suite. We are so excited to see your vision become an unforgettable reality at Valley Meadows!
