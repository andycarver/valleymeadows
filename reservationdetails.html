RENTAL PERIOD HOURS

- Friday, Saturday event rental periods end by 11:30pm, Sunday event rental period ends by 10:30pm
- Full Day event rental period must include (1) Setup: 2 hours setup before ceremony starts; (2) Cleanup: required 1 hour (caterer's use) in final hour.
- Full Day events may opt to add on additional hours at a discounted rate of $100 per hour (Full Day Rentals only)

FOR ALL EVENT RENTALS

- No Smoking in buildings or on patios. Smoking of tobacco products only, allowed only in designated areas
- You may bring your own beer & wine, or a bartender must be provided by the caterer. No friends/family bartending.
- Cash bars are not permitted
- Washington State Banquet Permit is required if alcohol is served. $11 fee.
- No corkage fee will be charged
- Event Liability Insurance recommended for all events, especially those where alcohol will be served
